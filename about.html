About Me

I started my professional career studying the engineering in Digital Systems and Robotics at the Tecnológico de Monterrey, campus state of México. During my Bachelor studies, I enrolled on different research groups of robotics such as the CEM robot research and development group and the Quantum Robotics team. Then, I began to focus my interests on artificial intelligence, particularly in machine learning and I started the Master in Computer Science. Here, I worked with Magnetic Resonance Spectroscopy signal analysis, focusing on reconstruction methods. I am currently a PhD student at the Tecnológico de Monterrey, working with Gravitational Wave signals from the LIGO Scientific Collaboration.
